What We Offer

Services Tailored to You

Every service at The Fade Lab is delivered with precision, care, and proper attention to detail.

Signature Fades

Our namesake service and the one we're known for across Geelong. From skin fades to mid and high tapers, Raj and Gary blend with surgical precision every single time.

Classic Men's Cuts

Not every bloke wants a fade, and that's more than fine. We handle everything from textured crops to slick business cuts — whatever suits your style and your lifestyle.

Kids' Haircuts

We've got young regulars like Henry who genuinely look forward to coming in. Our patient, relaxed approach means your little one will actually enjoy the experience — and you'll love the result.

Beard Shaping & Grooming

A great haircut deserves a beard that matches. We'll sculpt, trim, and tidy your facial hair so everything works together properly.

Hot Towel Finish

Every visit at The Fade Lab feels like more than just a quick chop. Our hot towel finish is the detail that turns a haircut into something you genuinely look forward to.
